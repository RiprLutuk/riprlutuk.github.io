Operational mindset

My strongest context is where backend, databases, security, and infrastructure meet.

01 · Reliability

Design for failure first

Connection pools, timeouts, retries, backups, restore drills, alerts, and fallback procedures matter as much as the happy path.

02 · Security

Small public surface, strong internal boundaries

Public entrypoints should be intentional. Internal services stay private. Credentials, scopes, and audit logs are first-class parts of the design.

03 · Simplicity

Tools must be easy to operate

I prefer clear runbooks, boring infrastructure, human-readable errors, and automation that reduces manual DBA work.
